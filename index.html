Crystal Game

There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. The player will be shown a random number at the start of the game. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. The amount of points is hiden until the player clicks a crystal. When they do click one, player's score counter will be updated with the number the crytal is worth. The player wins if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game.The player loses if their score goes above the random number. The game restarts whenever the player wins or loses. When the game begins again, each crystal will be worth a new random number. The app also displays the number of games the player wins and loses. To that end, do not refresh the page as a means to restart the game.

javascript, web, css, html
